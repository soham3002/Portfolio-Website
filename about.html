About Me

Hello! I'm Soham Rajam, a passionate Full Stack Web Developer with a deep love for coding. My journey in technology began at an early age, and since then, I have dedicated myself to mastering both front-end and back-end technologies. I believe that technology has the power to shape the future, and I strive to be a part of that transformation by creating innovative and functional web applications.

I am proficient in a variety of programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C++, and Python. Additionally, I have hands-on experience with Node.js, React, Express.js, and MongoDB, allowing me to work on both the client and server sides of applications. My skill set also includes experience with databases like MySQL and PostgreSQL.

My passion for coding isn't just about building products, but about solving real-world problems. I love crafting applications that are not only functional but also intuitive and user-friendly. I’m constantly exploring new technologies and frameworks to stay ahead of the curve and enhance the quality of the projects I work on.

In addition to my technical expertise, I have a strong interest in machine learning and natural language processing (NLP), which I actively study to incorporate advanced features into web applications. I aim to bridge the gap between traditional web development and the future of intelligent applications.

Beyond coding, I’m also a big advocate for clean and efficient code, as well as strong collaboration skills within development teams. I enjoy working in an agile environment, where I can contribute to every stage of development, from ideation to deployment.

When I’m not coding, you’ll likely find me exploring new tech trends, contributing to open-source projects, or attending hackathons and tech meetups. My ultimate goal is to work on projects that have a positive impact on people's lives and make the digital world a more connected place.
